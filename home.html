Welcome to the virtual 82nd Street from Jackson Heights, Queens.

Introduction

location_on

How can we extend the experience of urban life to those at a distance? This project will explore how to construct and interact with a virtual street mirroring a commercial stretch of 82nd Street in Jackson Heights, Queens. Through interacting with installed public seating in Jackson Heights and live footage, and story tour exhibition in Guggenheim, the people of different social groups, joining from different location will be more connected than ever.
